Chennai

Explore the cultural richness and beautiful beaches of Chennai.

Top Places to Visit

  • Marina Beach: One of the longest urban beaches in the world.
  • Kapaleeshwarar Temple: Ancient temple with Dravidian architecture.
  • Fort St. George: Historic fort built by the British in 1644.

Must-Try Dishes

  • Dosa: Crispy rice pancake served with chutney and sambar.
  • Idli: Steamed rice cakes, a popular South Indian breakfast.
  • Chettinad Chicken: Spicy chicken dish from Chettinad region.
