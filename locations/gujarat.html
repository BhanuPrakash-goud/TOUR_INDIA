Gujarat

Explore the land of heritage sites, vibrant culture, and delicious food.

Top Places to Visit

  • Rann of Kutch: Stunning white salt desert in northwest Gujarat.
  • Somnath Temple: Famous Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Shiva.
  • Gir National Park: Known for Asiatic lions and diverse wildlife.

Must-Try Dishes

  • Gujarati Thali: Variety of dishes served in a single platter.
  • Dhokla: Spongy, savory snack made from fermented rice and chickpeas.
  • Handvo: Savory vegetable cake made with rice and lentils.
