Kerala

Immerse in the serene backwaters and lush greenery of Kerala.

Top Places to Visit

  • Alleppey Backwaters: Known for houseboat cruises on scenic waterways.
  • Munnar: Beautiful hill station with tea plantations.
  • Thekkady: Wildlife sanctuary known for its elephant and tiger reserves.

Must-Try Dishes

  • Kerala Sadya: Traditional vegetarian feast served on a banana leaf.
  • Puttu and Kadala Curry: Steamed rice cake served with chickpea curry.
  • Appam with Stew: Rice pancake served with coconut-based vegetable stew.
