Mumbai

The city of dreams, known for Bollywood, beaches, and street food.

Top Places to Visit

  • Gateway of India: Historic arch monument overlooking the Arabian Sea.
  • Marine Drive: Scenic coastal road with views of the sea.
  • Elephanta Caves: Ancient rock-cut temples on Elephanta Island.

Must-Try Dishes

  • Vada Pav: Mumbai's famous spicy potato-filled bun.
  • Pav Bhaji: Mashed vegetables in a spicy gravy served with buttered buns.
  • Bhel Puri: Tangy and spicy puffed rice snack.
