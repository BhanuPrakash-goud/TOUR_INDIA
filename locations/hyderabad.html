Hyderabad

Explore the best of Hyderabad from historic monuments to the famous biryani.

Top Places to Visit

  • Charminar: Iconic mosque with stunning architecture.
  • Golconda Fort: A fort with rich history and panoramic views.
  • Ramoji Film City: One of the largest film cities in the world.

Must-Try Dishes

  • Hyderabadi Biryani: Aromatic rice dish with a blend of spices.
  • Haleem: Slow-cooked stew, popular during Ramadan.
  • Double Ka Meetha: Bread pudding dessert with nuts and saffron.
