Rajasthan

Discover the royal heritage, deserts, and forts of Rajasthan.

Top Places to Visit

  • Jaipur (Pink City): Known for Hawa Mahal and Amber Fort.
  • Udaipur: City of Lakes with stunning palaces.
  • Jaisalmer: Golden city with forts and sand dunes.

Must-Try Dishes

  • Dal Baati Churma: Traditional Rajasthani meal with wheat balls and lentils.
  • Laal Maas: Spicy mutton curry made with red chilies.
  • Ghewar: Sweet dessert popular during festivals.
