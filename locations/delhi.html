Delhi

Experience the blend of ancient and modern in India's capital city.

Top Places to Visit

  • India Gate: War memorial and iconic landmark in New Delhi.
  • Red Fort: Historic fort complex from the Mughal era.
  • Qutub Minar: UNESCO World Heritage Site, towering minaret.

Must-Try Dishes

  • Chole Bhature: Spicy chickpeas served with fried bread.
  • Butter Chicken: Creamy tomato-based chicken curry.
  • Paratha: Flaky, layered flatbread with various fillings.
