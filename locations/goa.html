Goa

Experience the sun, sand, and vibrant nightlife in Goa.

Top Places to Visit

  • Anjuna Beach: Famous for its scenic beauty and vibrant nightlife.
  • Bom Jesus Basilica: Historic church and UNESCO World Heritage Site.
  • Dudhsagar Falls: Majestic waterfall located on the Mandovi River.

Must-Try Dishes

  • Goan Fish Curry: Coconut-based fish curry with traditional Goan spices.
  • Vindaloo: Spicy and tangy pork dish influenced by Portuguese cuisine.
  • Bebinca: Popular Goan layered dessert made with coconut milk and ghee.
