Kolkata

Experience the cultural heritage and history of Kolkata.

Top Places to Visit

  • Victoria Memorial: Iconic white marble museum.
  • Howrah Bridge: Architectural marvel and popular landmark.
  • Indian Museum: Largest and oldest museum in India.

Must-Try Dishes

  • Rasgulla: Traditional Bengali dessert made of chhena and syrup.
  • Kathi Roll: Rolled paratha filled with spicy meat or veggies.
  • Macher Jhol: Fish curry served with rice.
